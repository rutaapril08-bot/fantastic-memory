Cat

Mingming

I love cats because there is something incredibly comforting about their presence—the way they quietly sit beside you, their soft fur, their curious eyes, and their unique personalities that make each one special. They can be playful and energetic one moment, then calm and affectionate the next, bringing both joy and peace into everyday life. I admire their independence, elegance, and mysterious nature, yet beneath that confident exterior, they are capable of forming deep bonds and showing genuine affection. Whether they're purring softly, following me around, or simply existing in their own little world, cats have a way of making me feel happy, understood, and at ease, which is why I love them so much.
